Introduction

Most of us are familiar with GPS, but most people don't know how it works. And for those who want to know, the online explanation is too complicated. So What I want to do is create a website that introduces GPS and briefly explains how it works so that you can understand it.

In this section, it will introduce the origin and development of GPS, and mainly introduce the three segments of GPS, and what devices or devices are in each consist of segment.
