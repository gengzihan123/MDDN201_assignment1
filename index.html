Global Position System

Most of us are familiar with GPS, but most people don't know how it works. And for those who want to know, the online explanation is too complicated. So What I want to do is create a website that introduces GPS and briefly explains how it works so that you can understand it.

Next

Content

For this project, I want to make a website that can introduce GPS, include introduction, working principle, and make a GPS mapthat can interacte with audience.

The introduction will cover the components of GPS as well as developments.

The working theory section will introduce how the satellite locates the equipment and explain the number of satellites required by the system.

For the GPS map part, I would like to make a map inside and try to connect with GPS so that people can interact with the map to better understand GPS.

Next

Animation

There is animation at the beginning of the page and when the page switches.

For the loading interface of the web page, I want to make an animation. First, the screen will be opened like an eye. Then a ball will appear in the middle to represent the earth. Finally, orbit and satellite will appear around it, and three sub-titles of the web page will appear below.

After that, when people jump to other page, it will close like close eyes, then there will be other images coming up and the content in the middle.
