GPS Map

Interacting with audience

Animation

Because this part is functionally different from the other two parts, when the audience clicks on the icon the website will slide down and show the map in the middle, and the map can normally work.

Logo

The Logo is designed according to the map marker and Google Map as the references. Considering that this part is to interact with the audience, this design is chosen to facilitate the audience to understand the purpose of this page.

Introduction

This part is to increase the interaction with the audience, so that the audience is more interested in GPS. In this section I added a Map to the page, which will connect to Google Map, where viewers can experience a similar function.
