Theory Page

This part is to explain GPS working theory

The theory part is the key part of this website. In order to make the audience better understand the content of this part, I will make some GIFs below to make it easy to understand.

In this part, Considering that the audience may not know much about physics, I will explain it in a simple way. The original introduction to waves, signals and coordinates is deleted, and the focus is on how the satellite is locate device. At the same time, it will say the GPS need at least four satellites for positioning, and then I will explain why four satellites are needed.

Interaction and animation

When audience clicks on the page, the background will change to a satellite, and the theory icon will move to the top left, others will be dark.

For this page interaction, when the audience click the icon on the home page, the icon at the bottom center moves to the top left, and the other two icons darken. At the same time, the background will close like eyes, then another background will come out, and the text will move from the bottom to the middle.
